INSTRUCTIONS:

You will take turns making choices during a group problem-solving game. Your options include:

  • Winning 3 tokens (exchangeable for 30¢) for yourself, OR
  • Winning 1 token (exchangeable for 10¢) for yourself, OR
  • Winning 1 token (exchangeable for 10¢) for yourself + donating 2 tokens (exchangeable for 20¢) toward the Western Energy Alliance

The Western Energy Alliance "believe that American oil and natural gas are an overwhelming force for good, as the foundation of human health, safety and welfare."

At some point, a new participant will replace you in the group. Then, you will receive real money based on your in-game choices. After the entire session has ended, we will donate real money toward the cause.
